  • Basics

  • What is Sareehouseonline?

    Sareehouseonline is the digital branch of SareeHouse outlet. It is an ecommerce platform that has in store a variety of sarees ,dresses and much more. Customers can place orders and have the products delivered to their address.

  • How do I change my password?

    If you want to change your password, please visit Login page and click on forgot password. Then, enter your email in the space provided and a link will be sent to that mail to set a new password.

  • How do I sign up?

    If you would like to Signup and create an account, click on the login/signup link given at the top menu. You would need to provide your Email id and set up a password along with a username to create and access the account.

  • Should I have an account in Sareehouseonline?

    An account in Sareehouse online is compulsory to access cart and place orders. However, even without an account you can view our products but cannot place them in cart.

  • How do i give a review/suggestion?

    You can visit the Contact Us page and write a review/suggestion to us.

  • Shopping

  • How do I buy products?

    To start buying products, you first need to login from your account(Please create one if you haven't created yet by Signing Up).After logging in,select the products you wish to buy and add them to your cart.After you have added the products to cart, visit the cart and click on Proceed to Checkout.Then, proceed to fill in the details required and at last, proceed to pay. Finally, after payment is done, your order will be delivered to you in a few days.You can also download the receipt after payment on the page.

  • How do I cancel my order?

    Please note that cancellations have to be made within 24 hours after placing an order. For cancelling your order you will have to visit Active Orders page and click on Cancel Order at the respective order (OR) you can get in touch with the customer support executive by sending a request via Contact Us page and giving your transaction id in the message.It may be noted that if cancellation of the order happens, then it attracts the payment gateway service charge, in such a case the respective service charge shall be deducted and the balance paid back to the customer. You may kindly note that order cannot be cancelled after 24 hours of placing order and once the shipment process has started from our end.

  • Checking out product categories

    You will see the widest range of Sarees, Dresses & many more Products with broad product categories on SareeHouse Pages, so you can click on the broad/sub classification of your choice and then find essential information relevant to the product. Browsing through categories also helps in narrowing down your choices.

  • What information is needed for checkout process?

    Checkout process requires basic information for better customer service and delivery such as personal details(Name,etc.) and Address for delivery. All fields must be compulsorily filled unless marked as optional.

  • What is shipping address?

    The shipping address is the address where you would like your order to be delivered.

  • What is billing address?

    The billing address is the address associated with your credit card if you are using a credit card for payment.The billing address must match the address that your credit card company has on file. It is generally not required unless specified by the credit card company.It is used to verify your authenticity of your credit card information.

  • Payments

  • What is the payment procedure?

    Please proceed to checkout page after you have added your items in the cart. After the checkout process is complete, you are redirected to a secure payment gateway where you are provided with multiple options to pay. Then, upon selecting the desired option, your payment will be complete and your order will be successfully placed.

  • What are the payment options available?

    A lot of payment options are made available on our payment gateway including credit cards,debit cards,net banking,etc. If you feel don't see your desired payment method available, please send us a message via Contact Us page or please call us using the numbers given at the bottom of the page.

  • Can I have the bill for my payment?

    Yes. You can definetely have it. The bill summary is immediately displayed on the page you are redirected to after the payment is successful. You can download it in pdf format by clicking the print button. Similar bill summary is also provided to you via mobile sms and email provided by you during the checkout process.

  • Why did my payment fail?

    The error might have occurred due to one of the following reasons-> Poor internet connectivity of the user,payment gateway maintenance period, Incorrect information provided by the user, User cancellation of the payment process,etc. Please try again after checking. Payment errors are possible but very rarely occur due to the error of the SareeHouse server. If errors continue to persist, you can contact through the various methods provided in the Contact Us page. We will look into the error.

  • Is there Cash On Delivery(COD) option available?

    Cash On Delivery(COD) option is currently unavailable. But, we are working on it to make it available to our customers soon.

  • Are there any additional taxes to be paid on the product?

    Please note that there are no additional taxes incurred on your bill summary. Taxes are already included in the product price.

  • What are the additional charges incurred during payment?

    Please note that there are no additional taxes incurred on your bill summary. Taxes are already included in the product price. There is a minimal fixed delivery fee of Rs.49 that is added to the bill to deliver the products to your home/office in Hyderabad. Delivery fee, therefore is the only additional charge added.

  • Privacy

  • How do we protect your information?

    When you place orders or access your account information, we offer the use of a secure server. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. Further more, all of the customer data we collect is protected against unauthorized access.

  • Sareehouseonline privacy guarantee

    Sareehouseonline.com does not rent, sell, share, barter or in any other way use personally-identifying information collected during your use of our Web site . From time to time we may reveal general statistical information about our Web site and visitors, such as number of visitors, number and type of goods and services purchased, etc., but we will never refer you by name or specific address. Your trust and confidence are our highest priority.

  • How is my payment data secure?

    Your payment data is handled by a trusted and secure payment gateway whose terms and privacy policy can be viewed at their website or information brochure. Your payment credentials are secure and not known to anyone except you.

  • How can I make sure no one else sees my cart and pages?

    Just click on the “Sign Out” link to sign out of SareeHouse’s personalized services. Signing out will allow you to change users and ensures that SareeHouse will ask for a user ID and password when someone visits SareeHouseonline. This way, no one will accidentally see your cart or pages.

  • Delivery

  • When is my order delivered?

    From the time on which an order has been successfully placed, it takes around 2-4 days for the processing and delivery to be completed.

  • What should I do if my order hasn't been delivered within 2-4 days?

    If your order has not been delivered withtin the maximum time frame specified, you may place a complaint to us through the form provided in the Contact Us page. Transaction ID must be compulsorily be provided in the form. Our team will look into it and see that the problem does not occur.

  • How do returns or refunds work?

    Refunds can be done in 3 scenarios as explained below:-
    1) If you have cancelled an order within 24 hours of placing it,then it attracts the payment gateway service charge and in such a case the respective service charge shall be deducted and the balance refunded to the customer.
    2) If there is an error during payment and amount has been deducted, your entire amount shall be refunded to you within a few days without any deductions. If it does not occur within 7 days, you may raise a complaint via Contact Us page.
    3) If you are not satisfied with the product after delivery due to some significant reason, you can return the product within 12 hours and money shall be refunded to you. If the reason is significant, no money shall be deducted and the entire amount shall be refunded back to you with respect to the product.Note:-You must first state the reason via Contact Us page along with transaction id (or) as a second option,you can call us to state the reason.

  • What products can i return?

    Only those products which are unused, unworn, unwashed and undamaged are eligible for return; discounted and/or altered products will not be eligible for a return. In the unlikely event that your merchandise has arrived in a damaged condition, you will be required to email us a photo of the damaged product within 12 hours of receiving your order at varshitht@gmail.com.

  • How is my order delivered?

    Your order is delivered to the address specified during the checkout process by a delivery person.

  • How is my order delivered?

    Your order is delivered to the address specified during the checkout process by a delivery person.

  • Disclaimer

    Please note that we have the right to refuse refund process if the reasons stated for requesting refund regarding the products are not significant or the products are used, worn, washed and damaged after delivery; discounted and/or altered products will not be eligible for a return. In the unlikely event that your merchandise has arrived in a damaged condition, you will be required to email us a photo of the damaged product within 12 hours of receiving your order at varshitht@gmail.com. If we are satisfied that sufficient reason exists, you will be refunded the entire amount within 2-7 days after we collect back the product.
